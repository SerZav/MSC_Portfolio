Microservices and Microkernels Debate

Comments on the debate

In this unit, we held a debate about future trends and some interesting concepts such as microservices and microkernels. I have worked in the software industry for a few years now, with more than 4 years as a professional developer, and I did not know the difference between a monolithic kernel vs the microkernel. Somehow this opened a new world for me since I strongly believe that microkernels will be very important in the near future because of the massive increase in cyberattacks and the rapid advance of technologies. For me, it is going to be a topic to consider for additional research and even as a dissertation theme. If it does not become the mainstream for operating systems, it should at least be an approach that many will consider for security purposes.

This unit was loaded with several emotions because on one hand, it was the end of a very intensive module where we had to catch up with several discussions and exercises left behind; and on the other hand, I felt that it was loaded with great content that I wished I have had more time to investigate. I strongly believe that the e-portfolio is going to be an invaluable tool for revisiting some of this content, and possibly for filling gaps where I wanted more information.
