Initial Post

In a study to examine the risk of a production training resource's security being compromised by attackers, several vulnerabilities were found without implementing sophisticated resources. In this study performed by students with basic knowledge and limited tools, a medical mannequin was successfully attacked, demonstrating a low level of security implemented to protect these devices (Glisson et al., 2015).

One of the vulnerabilities found in the security solution led to the successful use of a brute force attack to penetrate the internal router, which allowed access to the device. According to an alert from the Cybersecurity & Infrastructure Security Agency in 2012, access points that support WPS are sensible of being breached by attackers in a time frame between 4 and 10 hours with open-source tools (Cybersecurity & Infrastructure Security Agency, 2012). The steps to follow include updating the access point firmware and disabling the WPS. Therefore, it is essential to keep updated information on these alerts and reports about security.

Attacks on WPS/WPS2 security can be greatly enhanced by using dictionaries. One of the most important measures to increase protection against these attacks is to perform penetration tests, with tools that measure the weakness of the security solution (Ajay et al., 2021). WPS3 however, has a more robust protection against password dictionary attacks and other vulnerabilities and adoption of devices with this protocol is advised (Lounis & Zulkernine, 2019).

The second vulnerability found was related to the network protocol, which led to a denial of service, preventing the software to communicate with the mannequin. The most important measure to prevent DoS attacks or DDoS attacks in IoT devices is detection, for example monitoring network traffic. Other measures include prevention, by whitelisting the pre-approved devices allowed to connect to the device or implementing a firewall (Salim et al., 2020).

A well-designed and implemented software must be maintainable and extensible. It is an essential piece of an information system, along with the other components such as hardware, data, processes and people. They all must work highly coupled to provide a robust framework.

We came across several examples of how even a small component of an information system might cause a catastrophic failure. It is the responsibility of every member of a team to work with the strongest of ethics to provide a service with the highest standards. It is very important to distinguish when to implement OOP (Aniche et al., 2019).

To sum up, we have gained valuable knowledge about tools and methods that must be considered when participating in the software development lifecycle. We have a clear understanding of the importance of requirements gathering, testing and selecting the right methodology for implementation.

Basic principles of Object-Oriented Design

There are many advantages to this approach such as modularity, which makes it easier to maintain and expand, correct bugs, etc. OOD can improve reliability, flexibility and reusability. However, there are weaknesses if not used correctly or if the technique is abused, making the system hard and costly to integrate with other systems.

Abstraction: removes most superfluous details to leave the most important aspects of the object, so it is easier to use for other types. It represents a reusable, generic object.

Encapsulation: it is the concept of joining methods and states (functions and attributes) together in a class. The data is not accessed directly but through the methods inside a class.

Inheritance: having a master class (or super class), other objects can inherit its characteristics (parent to child inheritance). In this case we say that the child is coupled with its parent (super class). Any changes made to the super class, will be transferred to the sub-class.

Polymorphism: occurs when more than one object inherits from a parent class, overriding its default behaviours, while keeping the IS-A relationship with its parent. One object can then take on many forms.
