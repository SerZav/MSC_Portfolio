UNIT 11 - Future trends in Secure Software Development

The final assignment in unit 11 was an intensive test of teamwork. We could make a good distribution of tasks according to our skills, so we could fulfil every planned objective. Since we are all working in two assignments at the same time, we did not have enough time to complete other tasks or debates, especially for some of us that must share the time with a fulltime job. It has been a big challenge in terms of time management and resilience.

Thanks to the assignment and the teamwork, I had the opportunity to improve my Django skills. It was a big challenge to complete my part of the API because I started to work with this framework barely 3 weeks before. However, we held constant meetings during this week and kept a very fluid communication through several channels like Slack, which helped me gather the required knowledge to complete the task. At this point, I think I was lucky to have such a dedicated and supportive team.
