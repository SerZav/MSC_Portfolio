The formative activities were similar from those of other modules. However, I think it is important to delve deeper into the different options that we come across Software Development, and how to address them from the point of view of Project Management. I can relate to many of the problems derived of poor code quality, whether it is associated to bad documentation, poor project structure, or lack of standards.

An important challenge for me will be to keep in mind good practices to avoid technical-debt or scope creep from a developer’s perspective, when communication issues or lack of experience of a PM might not produce a good quality assurance plan. This awareness will help the team and keep me prepared for a technical lead position or even a PM role.

Write a set of non-functional requirements by which a software development may be tested to demonstrate that quality has been achieved.

Appreciate the ways in which technical debt may be accrued through ineffective software quality management.

Research topics

Examine the reasons for which effective quality management of a software development is important.

Explore a variety of metrics by which software quality may be measured.

Recognise the important roles to be played by quality management planning, control and assurance when working to provision a high-quality software product.
