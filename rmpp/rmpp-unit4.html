The literary review has been one of my ultimate discoveries during the module. It has been an underestimated tool, very famous but without practical use until now. Discussion between peers made apparent to me that it was a widespread feeling before this module. It is an academic instrument indispensable for research (Sajeevanie, 2021). However, reflecting on past units learning and getting involved with new ideas have clearly defined its relevance. I currently see it as a powerful tool to deliver proven knowledge to my workplace that will perfectly fit my role as an analyst. I would recommend additional guided discussion on the importance of literature review in the workplace between peers, but suggested reading and wider reading represented a solid entry route.
