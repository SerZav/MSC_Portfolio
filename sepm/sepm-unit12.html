UNIT 12 - The Case for the Future Direction of Software Engineering Project Management

The final assessment brought a huge amount of concepts together that felt a little overwhelming at first. The main challenge was to critically adapt the right concepts to our project, considering that in some aspects it is still a mock project.

After a long module, I see the first assignment with different perspective. I feel it lacks very important information that should be covered in an initial design, most of them part of the initial stages of a project (Such as dod, vision and scope, etc).

On specially challenging aspect of this module was to focus on project management. Many of the team members have a developer background or even other IT related background. This made it hard for everyone to assume the Project Manager role, trying to work more on the developer side of things or in other aspects such as presentation.
