Research Methods and Professional Practice

UNIT 10 - Research Writing

As a developer, it is natural to assume that academic research would be particularly challenging. However, I must undertake a lot of research during my daily activities and even conduct interviews and surveys. These activities are not academic-oriented, but they helped me feel more comfortable around these topics and facilitated the transition to a more academic point of view.

After a few weeks of building a research proposal, I consider that my perception about research has been profoundly impacted. My previous research experiences were limited to a few and unreliable sources. Practice and having the opportunity to analyse frameworks and methodologies increased my ability to go beyond and eventually improve my searching and classifying skills.
