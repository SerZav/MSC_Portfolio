UNIT 11 - Professional Development – Your e-Portfolio

One critical component of this module is the e-portfolio. After being in the computing industry for years and being interviewed several times for different roles, I have mixed opinions about its significance. I agree with its relevance for formative activities, improvement of self-reflecting capability and future reference of past learning (Eynon et al., 2014). However, in the professional field, I think it is best suited for junior roles (Ring et al., 2017).

Increasing the e-portfolio's applicability in a specialised role would require that it includes complex assignments or collaborative spaces such as open-source projects. In any case, part of my challenges after this course include improving it with new formative, filling voids I left behind and adding complex examples and scenarios.
