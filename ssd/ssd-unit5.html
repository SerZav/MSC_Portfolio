UNIT 5 - An Introduction to Testing

I have been programming for several years and testing has always been my main priority. I was familiar with most testing procedures that were introduced throughout this unit. However, it was a good opportunity to discuss new concepts such as Cyclomatic complexity, although I think that this topic did not get the attention of the participants. I would rather spend more time researching the SOLID principles, which more than a metric, is a method to improve code quality and extensibility.

During this unit, we spent a lot of time working as a team to develop the first module’s assignment. We had very productive meetings where we all had the opportunity to contribute with our best skills. However, working with a team represented a huge responsibility that I had to pair with my job. Moreover, I had to share the time with the rest of the activities of the two modules. This fact reduced my productivity and the amount of knowledge that I would have liked to acquire. Nevertheless, the e-portfolio can help me focus on some missing learning topics when I have time available… ¡I am already excited!
