Supermarket object model

The Unified Modelling Language has been helping companies to design object-oriented systems for many years. Before starting this module, I was under the impression that UML was obsolete. After discussions with out peers and researching through this unit, I can see that it is as important as it was a few years ago. The most important companies in the world still rely on UML and they will keep doing so. The practical activity was crucial to improve our learning and get a feedback from the tutor.

I enjoyed using different tools to design UML diagrams, but I chose Lucid Chart as my preferred tool because it is easy, intuitive and versatile. There are many concepts that are hard to master in UML, but I will work hard to get the most out of the resources available.
